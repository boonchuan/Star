Zones

Each player manages five core zones:

  • 🔄 Deck – Face-down draw pile.
  • 🤚 Hand – Cards available to play or set.
  • 🧍 Field – Allies and set traps in play.
  • 🗑️ Discard – Used or destroyed cards.
  • ⛔ Banish – Permanently removed from the game.
