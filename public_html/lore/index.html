🌌 Lore of Starfall Catastrophe

🌍 World Overview

The world of Starfall is fractured by a cosmic infection — a mutated fusion of disease and celestial energy. From this calamity rose six factions, each led by a hero forged in catastrophe.

⚔️ Factions

  • The Freeborn — Liberation through knowledge and rebellion.
  • Elemental Flame — Ritualists of embers and renewal.
  • Cradle of the Root — Symbiosis with ancient earth spirits.
  • Codex of the Eternal Spark — Archival mystics guarding lost code.
  • The Ember Ashes — Avatars of vengeance born in fire.
  • The Void Ones — Alien remnants of the cosmic rupture.
