Chapter 4: The Root Awakens

Beneath the jungles of D'rezzar, spores glowed with starlight. Virelia, once a biologist, now heard whispers in chlorophyll and decay. The shard had sunk into the world's mycelial network, awakening dormant intelligences older than speech.

The Cradle of the Root grew fast. Plants turned predatory, fungi sapient, vines writing equations in pollen. Virelia no longer served science—she served growth. The plague, to her, was nature’s reboot command. The Earth would live, with or without its former rulers.
