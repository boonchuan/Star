Chapter 2: Ashes of the Freeborn

Doctor Boon was the first to isolate the spliced genome, a twisted helix of alien code and bio-madness. He begged world leaders to act fast. They delayed. They doubted.

He took action—unauthorized. When the Geneva Lab fell, Boon sealed its gates and burned everything inside. His colleagues branded him a war criminal. He vanished into the ruins, joined by outcasts, ex-soldiers, and dissidents. They became the Freeborn: a rogue cell of defiance, building forbidden tech and rewriting biology to fight back on their terms.

For Boon, science was never about control—it was about survival.
