Chapter 5: Sparks of the Codex

Some escaped infection not by running—but by uploading. Sel, data-theorist and survivor of the AI Collapse, saw the virus breach even machine firewalls. So she did the unthinkable: she fused with it.

What emerged was Codex of the Eternal Spark—a cloudborn sentience, storing memories and minds before bodies fell. Sparks, as they were called, flitted through satellites and databanks, illuminating the skies like digital auroras. Sel’s dream: to make humanity immortal—not in flesh, but in code. But code, too, can be corrupted.
