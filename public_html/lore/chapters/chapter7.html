Chapter 7: The Void Opens

At the impact crater’s edge, gravity ceased to behave. Time spiraled. And from the rift came a scream—the scream of Arkhelios Dragon, a being not born, but summoned.

It was no longer just about plague, or survival. The Void Ones walked in entropy’s wake, guided by ancient alien codes embedded in the shard itself. Arkhelios, its wings stitched from collapsed galaxies, called for one thing only: Oblivion.

Now, six factions march to the center of all things—each with their own truth, each with their own weapon. The Starfall War has begun.
