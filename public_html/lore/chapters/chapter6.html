Chapter 6: Infernos Reborn

Ember Glow rose from betrayal. Once a general, left to die in a viral firestorm, she emerged with her skin charred and her heart forged anew. Those who followed her—wounded, discarded, angry—formed the Ember Ashes.

They didn't want peace. They wanted retribution. Cities that turned them away burned. Outposts that denied them medicine crumbled. Their creed was simple: If the world won’t save us, we’ll raze it until it begs for forgiveness.
