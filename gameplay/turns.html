Turn Flow

Each turn consists of five structured phases:

  1. Draw Phase: Draw 1 card. If your deck is empty, you lose.
  2. Energy Phase: Gain energy equal to 3 + ceil(M / 2).
  3. Play Phase: Play cards and set traps.
  4. Battle Phase: Attack with allies. Resolve trap triggers.
  5. End Phase: Cleanup, discard, and reset for the next turn.
